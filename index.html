Who we are

Computer Society of India ( CSI) is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. It was started on 6 March 1965 by a few computer professionals and has now grown to be the national body representing computer professionals.

The organisation has grown to an enviable size of 100,000 strong members consisting of professionals with varied backgrounds including Software developers, Scientists, Academicians, Project Managers, CIO’s , CTO’s & IT vendors to just name a few.

CSI SB GECT has started on 6 March 2018 with an aim to develop young and passionate students and make them industry ready. We have a network of 1000+ students and a great pack of mentors so far.
